AI Sevak Portal

A unified, AI-assisted operations platform to streamline volunteer coordination, improve governance, and scale seva impact with clarity.

1) Problem Statement

As seva initiatives expand, coordination remains fragmented across manual flows, disconnected communication channels, and repeated onboarding efforts.

  • Volunteer intake and allocation are inconsistent and difficult to track.
  • Updates are scattered across informal channels, causing visibility gaps.
  • Leaders spend high time on repetitive onboarding and follow-ups.
  • Unstructured data limits reporting quality and impact storytelling.

2) Objective

Build one internal platform that standardizes the complete volunteer lifecycle while preserving a human-led, AI-assisted operating model.

Operational Goals

  • Reduce onboarding and allocation turnaround time.
  • Centralize communication, task updates, and evidence.
  • Enable role-based governance and approvals.

Experience Goals

  • Improve volunteer clarity from registration to execution.
  • Provide timely context for new and mid-cycle joiners.
  • Create reliable visibility for C1, C2, C3, and C4 roles.

3) Proposed Solution

AI Sevak Portal brings registration, allocation, onboarding, collaboration, logs, and expense workflows into one structured system.

Core Modules

  • Profile setup and seva discovery
  • Structured onboarding and progress briefings
  • Calendar, hour logging, and seva messaging
  • Logs moderation and transparent expense tracking

AI Assistance

  • Seva recommendation by skills and availability
  • Onboarding Q&A from approved internal content
  • Progress summarization for leaders and teams

Governance

  • Role-based access (C1/C2/C3/C4 + Admin)
  • Human review before official story publishing
  • Audit-friendly decision and moderation trails

4) Technical Approach

Delivery follows a phased architecture: mock-first validation, then robust backend and database integration for production reliability.

Platform Stack

  • Frontend: Next.js + TypeScript + Tailwind
  • Backend: NestJS + TypeScript APIs
  • Data Layer: PostgreSQL + Prisma
  • Security Model: Auth + RBAC + audit controls

Execution Plan

  • Phase 1: MVP operations baseline
  • Phase 2: collaboration and moderated logs
  • Phase 3: AI assistance and pilot rollout
  • Phase 4+: stabilization and scaling readiness

5) Expected Impact & Future Scope

The portal is designed to convert fragmented operations into a repeatable, transparent, and scalable seva execution model.

Near-Term Impact

  • Faster volunteer-to-seva matching and onboarding
  • Reduced manual coordination load for leaders
  • Consistent logs for reporting and visibility
  • Better decision support through structured data

Future Scope

  • Wider rollout across additional seva programs
  • Enhanced analytics and capacity planning dashboards
  • Stronger donation-to-impact storytelling workflows
  • Continuous refinement with pilot feedback loops
